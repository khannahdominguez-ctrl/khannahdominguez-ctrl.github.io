Cycle 101

The menstrual cycle includes different phases that influence energy, mood, pain levels, and overall health. Understanding these phases can help identify patterns that feel normal and patterns that may need more attention.

The Bloody Red Flags

These signs can be helpful starting points for a conversation with a healthcare provider. Everyone is different, but these patterns may signal something worth checking out.
