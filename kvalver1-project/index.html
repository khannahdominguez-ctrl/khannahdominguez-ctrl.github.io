Why Female Health Education Matters

There is a major lack of education surrounding female anatomy, especially when it comes to menstrual health. Many people do not know that their periods are irregular, or that painful symptoms are not always normal. While technology continues to advance, healthcare advocacy for women is still a serious issue. People deserve to understand their bodies and have more options than just being prescribed birth control.
